BerryBreach

Your Utility for Animal Jam Classic Account Verification

About BerryBreach

BerryBreach is a desktop utility software designed to assist users of the game Animal Jam Classic in verifying the status and membership details of their game accounts. Our application provides a straightforward way for players to manage and confirm their account information.

We aim to provide a helpful tool for the Animal Jam Classic community, facilitating easier account information management for legitimate users.

How It Works

  • Account Input: Users provide their Animal Jam Classic account credentials (username and password) directly into the secure desktop application.
  • Verification: BerryBreach then attempts to verify the account status and membership details with Animal Jam Classic's authentication system.
  • Results: The application displays the results, indicating if an account is valid and its current membership status.
  • Credit System: To use the verification features, users can utilize daily free checks or purchase credit packages. Each account check consumes one credit.

Purchasing Credits

Users can purchase credit packages securely through Stripe Checkout to continue using the verification features beyond their daily free allowance. This ensures a safe and reliable payment process.

Note: BerryBreach is intended for use with accounts that you own or have explicit permission to access. Users are responsible for adhering to the Terms of Service of Animal Jam Classic.
